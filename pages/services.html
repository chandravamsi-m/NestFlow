Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about NestFlow's features? Browse our detailed FAQ sections below to learn more about our processes.

What is your tenant vetting process?
Our screening process is comprehensive, involving legal background checks, credit scoring audits, current employment verification, and tenant rental history reviews. This rigorous check reduces vacancies and ensures maximum tenant retention.
How are emergency property maintenance requests handled?
Tenants can report emergency repairs 24/7 directly through the mobile app or tenant portal. Our automated system matches the request with our vetted local technician pool and dispatches help within a standard 2-hour window.
How do smart contracts and ledger payments work?
NestFlow utilizes secure digital ledgers for absolute lease safety. Contracts are signed electronically and rent is routed directly from the tenant's ledger card to the owner's bank account, creating an immutable audit trail.
Are there any hidden broker or listing termination fees?
Absolutely not. We prioritize total pricing transparency. Our fee structure details security deposit escrows and utility rates upfront. There are no hidden list creation fees or contract termination penalties.
