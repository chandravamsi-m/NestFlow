About NestFlow

Reimagining property management and real estate discovery through innovative technology and exceptional service.

Our Purpose

Our Mission to Simplify Real Estate

Founded in 2026, NestFlow emerged from a simple observation: property management shouldn't be complicated. We set out to build a platform that seamlessly connects property owners, managers, and tenants under one intuitive ecosystem.

Today, we manage thousands of premium properties across multiple metropolitan areas, providing an unparalleled digital experience combined with exceptional human support. We believe that finding and renting a home should feel like flow, not friction.

Our Growth

Our Journey & Milestones

A look back at how we have scaled our services and platform to transform real estate management.

2020
2020

The Founding Vision

NestFlow was founded in a modest studio by our CEO, Sarah Jenkins, with a core team of three engineers. The mission was clear: replace paper lease forms and phone-tag scheduling with a single responsive dashboard.

2022
2022

Expanding to 10+ Cities

After raising our Series A funding, we expanded our platform operations across 10 major cities, including Seattle, Miami, and Brooklyn, onboarding over 1,000 active landlord accounts.

2024
2024

$2 Billion in Asset Value

NestFlow's platform transactions officially crossed $2 Billion in property asset value. We launched our customizable client portal and automated rent distribution system.

2026
2026

Next-Gen Real Estate Flow

Relaunching the NestFlow portal with AI-powered neighborhood matches, visual lightbox gallery tours, interactive budget estimators, and full dark-mode responsive support.
