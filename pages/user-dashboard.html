Tenant Maintenance Guidelines

What constitutes a Maintenance Emergency?

Emergency issues are those that pose an immediate threat to your safety or the property structure. Routine requests (like slow drains, window lock fixes, cabinet adjustments) are generally scheduled and solved within 24 to 48 hours.
